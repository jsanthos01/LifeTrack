About

Lose your weight over a chat and socializing! At Fitness Diaries, we believe staying healthy and losing weight need not be a lonely and exhausting journey. Fitness Diaries adds a twist to your fitness routine and makes it fun and competitive. Create your fitness group, add your friends, track your goal to challenge each other and make it fun. Also, the app gives you trending diets and recipes and exercises to follow to achieve your goal.
